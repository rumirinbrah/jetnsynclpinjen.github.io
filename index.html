Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 January 2026

JetnSync Studio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Vinyl Records Management app (the “App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

All data created or added by the user — including vinyl record details and images selected from the device gallery — is stored locally on the user’s device only.

The App does not require user accounts, does not use analytics or tracking tools, does not access the internet, and does not transmit data to any remote server.

Images and Media Access

The App allows users to select images from their device gallery using the system image picker.

  • Image access is user-initiated
  • Selected images are stored only within the app’s internal storage
  • Images are not shared, uploaded, or accessed outside the device

The App does not request any storage or media permissions.

Data Sharing

The App does not share data with any third parties. No third-party services, SDKs, or libraries are used to collect or process user data.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on the device and never transmitted, users retain full control over their information. Deleting the App will remove all associated locally stored data.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
